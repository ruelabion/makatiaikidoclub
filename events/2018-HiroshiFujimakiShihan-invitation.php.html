Event Details

We cordially invite all aikidoists and prospective aikido students to train with us at the 2018 annual seminar of Aikido Philippines (AP), the Filipino Federation of Aikido (FFA), and the Pilipinas Aikido Propagation Association (PAPA).

Hiroshi Fujimaki Shihan, 7th dan, from Aikido World Headquarters in Tokyo, shall lead the seminar.

The two-day seminar shall be on 2018 September 08 - 09 (Saturday-Sunday), at the San Lorenzo Village covered court, San Lorenzo Drive, San Lorenzo Village, Makati City.

There will be one 90-minute session in the morning, and two 90-minute sessions in the afternoon of both days. A portion of the last session of September 08 shall be given over to a demonstration (enbukai) by senior Filipino aikidoists, and the last session of September 09 will be a promotion examination for qualified candidates.

A traditional farewell dinner will be held on the evening of September 09. Everyone is welcome to this dinner.

For full details on seminar contribution amounts, schedules, venue location map, mechanics of contribution remittance, and others, please visit the Home page ofwww.makatiaikidoclub.comand click on the image of the poster for the event.
