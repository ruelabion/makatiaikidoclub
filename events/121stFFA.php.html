Event Details


2011 July 02 121st FFA monthly interclub practice


All are invited to the 121st FFA monthly interclub Aikido practice on Saturday, 2011 July 02. This series of joint monthly practice sessions started in 2000.

DETAILS:

MAT CONTRIBUTION:
PhP100

DATE AND TIME:
Saturday, 2011 July 02
1830 hrs (6:30 p.m.)

THIS MONTH'S HOST:
Jitsuyo Bugei Aikido Club

THIS MONTH'S VENUE:
Club 650
C-5 Road, Libis, Pasig City*

ALL REGARDLESS OF ORGANIZATIONAL AFFILIATION ARE WELCOME. For more details, e-mail macaikido@yahoo.com or call 890-1976 or 77.

* Getting there: STREET DIRECTIONS

A) DRIVING YOUR CAR

Coming from the south (Makati/The Fort/Pasig/Ortigas Ave. etc), Club 650 is on the right, one lot beyond Shopwise and several lots before the entrance to Eastwood. Coming from the the north (UP/Ateneo/Blue Ridge area), take C-5 into Libis area and make a u-turn a few hundred meters after the Jollibee/Delifrance outlet. There is ample parking in front.

B) USING PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION

Club 650 is accessible via the jeeps that traverse C-5. From EDSA, get off at the Ortigas intersection, take a jeep to the Ortigas-C-5 intersection, get off at C-5 and take a second jeep north towards Eastwood. Club 650 will be on the right coming from the south (Makati/Pasig/Ortigas Ave.), and immediately beside Shopwise and before the entrance to Eastwood.
