Event Details

2023 June 24 - 25: MAC 40th Anniversary seminar



Annual Seminar:
After a three-year interruption occasioned by the COVID-19 pandemic, the Philippine aikido community resumes taking instruction from a Japan Aikikai master.

Featured again to teach this year is Fujimaki Hiroshi Shihan, 7th dan.

The two-day seminar will be held at the covered court of the St. Camillus Center for Humanization in Health, in Varsity Hills Village, Loyola Heights, Quezon City. It will be the first time an aikido seminar will be held at that venue.

2023 also marks the 40th anniversary of the Makati Aikido Club. The MAC has operated nonstop since 1983, including during the COVID lockdowns, during which it conducted 269 Zoom classes under Troy Nones Shidoin.

A) Lodging at seminar premises:
For those who wish to stay overnight during the seminar days, St. Camillus Center has 13 3-person rooms and 11 single/doubles. First come first served. Seminar participants who want to book a room should get in touch directly with Mr. Karl Martinez of St. Camillus Center. His contact numbers are 8426-1966 (landline) and 0917-5LISTEN (cellphone). Call during office hours.

St. Camillus is located at 18 Nicanor Reyes St., Varsity Hills Village, Loyola Heights, Quezon City. See map below.

B) Lodging off seminar premises:
Out-of-town and overseas seminar participants who are unable to secure one of the limited rooms at St. Camillus can find AirBnB's and hotels in the general vicinity.
