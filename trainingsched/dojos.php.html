The above training schedule table is kept scrupulously updated. However, it goes without saying that no training is held on Christmas Eve; Christmas day; New Year's Day; Maundy Thursday through Easter Sunday; and All Saints' Day. On other holidays, training continues unless specifically cancelled. The MAC also observes a 30-plus-years tradition of training every New Year's Eve, from 1400 - 1600 hrs.
